Glimpses into the Divine

The scriptures teach us that God made us and put us here on Earth, so to develop a philosophy of life, I only needed to look into God’s purpose of putting us here. Below are some scriptures that talk about God's purposes and are foundational to my understanding and beliefs about life. To summarize, they teach that life is about using our agency, preparing to meet and become like God, and finding joy. These purposes are accomplished through a mortal testing ground built for us to grow and test us on our obedience to God's directions. They have the goal of helping us grow into something better than what we are now.

2 Nephi 2:25 “Adam fell that men might be; and men are, that they might have joy.”

2 Nephi 2:27 “Wherefore, men are free according to the flesh; and all things are given them which are expedient unto man. And they are free to choose liberty and eternal life, through the great Mediator of all men, or to choose captivity and death, according to the captivity and power of the devil;…”

Alma 34:32 “For behold, this life is the time for men to prepare to meet God; yea, behold the day of this life is the day for men to perform their labors.”

Moses 1: 39 “For behold, this is my work and my glory—to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man.”
